<h2>How Arbitrage Works in Our System: Step-by-Step</h2>

<ol style="max-width: 800px; margin: 2em auto; font-size: 1.1em; line-height: 1.8; color: var(--text-secondary);">
  <li><strong>Spot Opportunities:</strong> Identify price differences across global markets, just like standard arbitrage.</li>
  <li><strong>Enter and Grab:</strong> Execute trades to capture the imbalance.</li>
  <li><strong>Redirect Instead of Exit:</strong> Flow the results into the CYPU - CYPUV pool for community benefit.</li>
  <li><strong>Cycle Back:</strong> Outputs feed into the WKAS pool on Kaspacom DEX, maintaining value and building volume.</li>
  <li><strong>Long-Term Growth:</strong> Over time, this accumulates automatically, strengthening the entire system.</li>
</ol>
